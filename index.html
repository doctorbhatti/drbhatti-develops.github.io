RxJava for Android

Basics about RxJava specifically in terms of Android.

RxJava a.k.a Reactive Java are the pack of new reactive extensions for Java. Named reactive, due to their behaviour and working. Basically in RxJava asynchronous tasks runs on different threads, ultimately producing desired output. It is done with the help of four types of members i.e. Observables, Observers, Subscribers & Schedulers(important in Android). Their further details will be shared in upcoming blogs of this series.
